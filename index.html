Owan Trust Energy

Owan Trust Energy, based in Ojavun, Owan East, Edo State, Nigeria, specializes in selling and refilling branded gas cylinders, alongside offering efficient gas stoves, burners, and various kitchen accessories. Emphasizing customer engagement through a subscription-based model and network marketing, the company caters to a growing market with a focus on innovation and community engagement.
