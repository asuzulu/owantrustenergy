Owan Trust Energy:

Powering Lives with Innovation and Reliability.

Located in Ojavun, Owan East, Edo State, Nigeria, Owan Trust Energy emerges as a frontrunner in the energy sector, specializing in the sale and refilling of gas cylinders. Its portfolio extends to high-quality gas stoves and burners, developed through collaborations with top engineers, and an array of essential accessories like hoses and control valves. Branded with unique serial numbers, their gas cylinders stand out for enabling efficient tracking and fostering customer loyalty.
